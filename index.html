1968

Intel is Founded

Beginning of Intel’s innovation journey.

Intel begins setting the foundation for technological advancement, which would later incorporate sustainable practices.

2003

First Sustainability Report

Intel releases its first corporate responsibility report.

This report outlines Intel’s environmental goals and community engagement efforts, launching a new era of accountability.

2012

100% Conflict-Free Microprocessors

Intel achieves 100% conflict-free validation for key minerals in its microprocessors.

Intel becomes the first company to produce microprocessors validated as conflict-free for tantalum, tin, tungsten, and gold.

2020

2030 RISE Strategy Announced

Intel announces its RISE strategy for 2030.

The RISE framework focuses on responsibility, inclusion, sustainability, and enabling technology to solve global challenges.
